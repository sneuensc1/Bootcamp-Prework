A Belle and Sebastian Tribute Page

Bio

Belle and Sebastian are a Scottish indie pop band. They were founded in Glasgow in the 1990s but Stuart Murdoch and Stuart David. They have had 10 members over the course of their existence, with different members coming and going. Stuard Murdoch has been there since the inception and continues to lead the band. Stuard David left in 2000. The band has exciting live shows with lots of lights and visuals to go along with the music. The band has 10 albums in total over the course of their existence.
